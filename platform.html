Platform Overview

It connects the customer (front office) to operations (back office). And it does this in a way that you can control and automate regulated conversations at scale.

Conversational AI Agents that get the job done, first time

Trust Orchestrator allows you to build and deploy Conversational AI Agents capable of getting the job done, no matter the call complexity.

1

Routing

Clarifying and categorising the caller's intent before routing the engagement through to the relevant system or person to resolve

2

3

Responding in context of the situation, data and rules

5

Actioning

Triggering the relevant systems to perform the required next action

6

Processing

Talking through a specific process and dynamically adjusting to the situation, data and rules

7

Tracking

Giving details of every conversation they had. Every question asked, rule applied, answer given and action taken for insights and compliance

Engineer trust into every conversation

Trust Orchestrator consists of 3 modules that work in concert to orchestrate high volumes of sales, service and support conversations across multiple channels and systems, in line with business and regulatory rules.

Trust Studio

Trust Studio allows low/no code teams to define the rules of the conversational guardrails for targeted conversations

  • Architect trusted conversations
  • Data-driven logic objects
  • Low/no code interface
  • Reusable conversation flows

Trust Engine

Trust Engine applies the rules in context to each conversation and orchestrates the correct next response or action

  • Power conversations in context
  • Rich APIs and connectors
  • Real-time data integration
  • Context compilation

Trust Manager

Trust Manager controls the rules for access rights, global settings, reporting and logic maintenance

  • Control conversations at scale
  • Access rights management
  • Version control
  • Reporting configuration

Trust Orchestrator works within existing CX stacks

Trust Orchestrator is designed to be agnostic of channel and system. Its role is to control the flow of every conversation across channels and systems, while keeping a detailed record of everything that is said or done.
