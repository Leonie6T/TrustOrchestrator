Introducing Call Navigator

The bridge between full automation and human connection. Designed as part of Trust Orchestrator's mission to fully automate rule-bound conversations, it empowers human agents to step in seamlessly when empathy matters most.

When customers prefer to speak with a person, Trust Orchestrator ensures your human agents perform at their best. Call Navigator dynamically guides agents through every step of the conversation, adapting to context, maintaining compliance, and providing instant answers when needed. It also reduces training demands by guiding agents through unfamiliar processes in real time, allowing them to step in confidently whenever a customer prefers a human touch.

Agents handle complex, context-rich conversations confidently while AI manages the rules, structure, and accuracy behind the scenes.
